These records make glaciers key archives for understanding past environmental conditions.

As ice layers degrade, the integrity of these climatic records is compromised, limiting the extent to which scientists can accurately interpret past environmental conditions.

Consequently, glaciers are not only indicators of climate change but also irreplaceable repositories of Earth 's environmental history. ❄️🌍
